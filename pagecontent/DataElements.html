AGE >= 66 years
AND
>= 1 ENCOUNTER DIAGNOSES include `CODES TO IDENTIFY FRAILTY`
AND EITHER
>= 1 ENCOUNTER of type acute inpatient where DIAGNOSES include `CODES TO IDENTIFY ADVANCED ILLNESS`

>= 2 ENCOUNTERS of type outpatient, observation, ED or nonacute inpatient where DIAGNOSES include `CODES TO IDENTIFY ADVANCED ILLNESS`
