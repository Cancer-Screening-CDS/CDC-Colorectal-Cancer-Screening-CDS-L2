  3. Determine Next Due Date for Screening (Multi-Society Task Force)

Description

This logic path calculates the next screening due date based on the most recent screening test modality and date, per recommendations from the U.S. Multi-Society Task Force for Colorectal Cancer (USMSTF) for patients at average risk of developing colorectal cancer.
