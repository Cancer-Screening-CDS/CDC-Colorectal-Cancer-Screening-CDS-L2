Description

This logic path provides recommendations for colorectal cancer surveillance in individuals with hereditary syndromes which carry an increased risk of colorectal cancer.

Actions

Recommendation: Refer to GI specialist for colonoscopy surveillance recommendations
Description
RECOMMENDATION:
Patients with a hereditary cancer syndrome require specialized management and colonoscopy surveillance for colorectal cancer (as well as additional surveillance for other cancers). Recommendations on when to start surveillance and surveillance intervals vary according to the syndrome.
Recommendation: Refer to GI specialist for colonoscopy surveillance recommendations
Description
RECOMMENDATION:
Refer to GI specialist for colorectal cancer (and possibly additional cancers) surveillance recommendations. Patients with a family history of hereditary cancer syndromes are often surveilled with the same frequency as patients with confirmed syndromes.
Recommendation: Consider referral to genetic counseling
Description
RECOMMENDATION:
Consider referral to genetic counseling for further evaluation and possible genetic testing.
Recommendation: Obtain comprehensive family history
Description
RECOMMENDATION:
Obtain comprehensive family history.
