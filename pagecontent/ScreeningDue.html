Due for screening?
Refer to next due date, which is the output of previous pathways involving assessment of patient history and risk factors, to determine whether patient is currently due for screening study, overdue or not yet due.
OVERDUE if next due date >= 6 months ago
DUE if next due date within 3 months from now OR < 6 months ago
Otherwise NOT DUE
